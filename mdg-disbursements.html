Disbursement Policy

The rules that govern travelling and other claims. Please read before submitting.

1. Who may claim

Any Master Drilling employee may submit a disbursement claim for legitimate, work-related expenses incurred on behalf of the company. There is no limit on how often claims may be submitted.

2. Travelling claims

Travel is reimbursed per kilometre at the prevailing company rate. Each trip must record the date, the from and to points, and the distance. Only business travel qualifies; daily home-to-office commuting does not.

3. Other claims

Out-of-pocket expenses (accommodation, materials, sundries, and similar) must each carry a date and a clear description. Supporting receipts must be retained and attached.

4. Approval

Every claim requires sign-off from the employee's Head of Department or Site Manager before it reaches Finance for payment. Claims without approval will not be processed.

5. Submission deadlines

Claims should be submitted promptly, ideally within the same month the expense was incurred, to keep monthly reporting accurate.

6. Payment

Approved claims are processed by Finance and paid into the employee's banking account through the normal payment run.
